Azure Lab Setup for AZ-104: Identity and Governance Practice

This lab provides hands-on experience with managing identities and governance in Azure, aligning with the AZ-104 exam objectives. You'll create and configure various Azure AD and governance components, simulate real-world scenarios, and verify configurations. The lab assumes you have an Azure subscription with sufficient permissions (e.g., Global Administrator or User Administrator for identity tasks, and Owner/Contributor for governance tasks).
